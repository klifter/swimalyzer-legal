Privacy Policy Last updated: December 2025 Summary: SwimAlyzer stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information with third parties. 1. Introduction SwimAlyzer ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our swimming technique analysis application. 2. Information We Collect 2.1 Data Stored Locally All data is stored exclusively on your device: Videos: Swimming videos you record or import for analysis Analysis Results: Technique metrics and scores generated by the app Settings: Your language preference and app settings History: Your saved analysis history 2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect Personal identification information (name, email, address) Location data Health data beyond swimming technique analysis Biometric data Financial information (handled by Apple) 3. Data Storage & Security Your data never leaves your device: No cloud uploads No server-side storage No data transmission to third parties Data is protected by your device's security features 4. Third-Party Services 4.1 Apple App Store Purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple handles all payment information according to their privacy policy: apple.com/legal/privacy 4.2 RevenueCat We use RevenueCat for subscription management. RevenueCat processes anonymized purchase data to manage your subscription status. See their privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy 4.3 Analytics We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve the app. This data cannot identify you personally and includes: App feature usage patterns Crash reports General device type (not unique identifiers) 5. Your Rights You have full control over your data: Access: All your data is accessible directly in the app Delete: Delete the app to remove all locally stored data Export: You can export your analysis videos through standard iOS sharing 6. Children's Privacy SwimAlyzer is intended for users aged 13 and older (or 16 and older in the EU without parental consent). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages. All data remains local on the device. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at feedback@swimalyzer.com. 7. International Users Since data is stored locally on your device, no international data transfers occur through our app. 8. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or App Store update notes. 9. GDPR Compliance (EU Users) For users in the European Union: We operate on the legal basis of contract performance and legitimate interest No personal data is transferred outside your device Right to Access: All your data is visible in the app Right to Rectification: You can edit your saved analyses Right to Erasure: Delete the app to remove all data Right to Data Portability: Export videos via iOS sharing Right to Object: You can opt out of anonymous analytics in Settings 10. CCPA Compliance (California Users) For California residents under the California Consumer Privacy Act: Right to Know: All your data is stored locally and visible in the app Right to Delete: Delete the app to remove all data No Sale of Data: We do not sell personal information Non-Discrimination: We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights To exercise your rights, you may delete the app or contact us at the email below. 11. Contact Us If you have questions about this Privacy Policy: Email: feedback@swimalyzer.com
