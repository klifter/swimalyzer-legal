Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Summary: SwimAlyzer stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information with third parties. We collect anonymous, non-personal scoring data to improve our algorithms.

1. Introduction

SwimAlyzer ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our swimming technique analysis application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally

All data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Videos: Swimming videos you record or import for analysis
  • Analysis Results: Technique metrics and scores generated by the app
  • Settings: Your language preference and app settings
  • History: Your saved analysis history

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Health data beyond swimming technique analysis
  • Biometric data
  • Financial information (handled by Apple)

3. Data Storage & Security

Your personal data never leaves your device:

  • No cloud uploads of videos or personal data
  • No server-side storage of personal information
  • No data transmission of personal information to third parties
  • Data is protected by your device's security features

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Apple App Store

Purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple handles all payment information according to their privacy policy: apple.com/legal/privacy

4.2 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat for subscription management. RevenueCat processes anonymized purchase data to manage your subscription status. See their privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

4.3 Analytics

We collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve the app. This data cannot identify you personally and includes:

  • App feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports
  • General device type (not unique identifiers)

4.4 Algorithm Improvement Data

To improve our scoring algorithms, the app transmits anonymous analysis metrics after each swimming analysis. This data includes:

  • Scoring values and technique metrics (e.g., entry angle, recovery height)
  • Video metadata (duration, resolution, camera angle)
  • Pose detection quality indicators

This data does not include:

  • Your name, profile, or any personal identifiers
  • Videos or images
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses

This anonymous data cannot be linked to you and is used solely to calibrate and improve scoring accuracy for all users.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible directly in the app
  • Delete: Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Export: You can export your analysis videos through standard iOS sharing

6. Children's Privacy

SwimAlyzer is intended for users aged 13 and older (or 16 and older in the EU without parental consent). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages. All data remains local on the device.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at feedback@swimalyzer.com.

7. International Users

Since personal data is stored locally on your device, no international transfers of personal data occur through our app. Anonymous algorithm improvement data (Section 4.4) is transmitted to servers in the European Union.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or App Store update notes.

9. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union:

  • We operate on the legal basis of contract performance and legitimate interest
  • No personal data is transferred outside your device
  • Anonymous algorithm improvement data (Section 4.4) is processed on the legal basis of legitimate interest and stored on servers within the EU
  • Right to Access: All your data is visible in the app
  • Right to Rectification: You can edit your saved analyses
  • Right to Erasure: Delete the app to remove all data
  • Right to Data Portability: Export videos via iOS sharing
  • Right to Object: You can opt out of anonymous analytics in Settings

10. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

For California residents under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: All your data is stored locally and visible in the app
  • Right to Delete: Delete the app to remove all data
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell personal information
  • Non-Discrimination: We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights

To exercise your rights, you may delete the app or contact us at the email below.
