Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Summary: SwimAlyzer stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information with third parties.

1. Introduction

SwimAlyzer ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our swimming technique analysis application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally

All data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Videos: Swimming videos you record or import for analysis
  • Analysis Results: Technique metrics and scores generated by the app
  • Settings: Your language preference and app settings
  • History: Your saved analysis history

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Health data beyond swimming technique analysis
  • Biometric data
  • Financial information (handled by Apple)

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data never leaves your device:

  • No cloud uploads
  • No server-side storage
  • No data transmission to third parties
  • Data is protected by your device's security features

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Apple App Store

Purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple handles all payment information according to their privacy policy: apple.com/legal/privacy

4.2 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat for subscription management. RevenueCat processes anonymized purchase data to manage your subscription status. See their privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

4.3 Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve the app. This data cannot identify you personally and includes:

  • App feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports
  • General device type (not unique identifiers)

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible directly in the app
  • Delete: Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Export: You can export your analysis videos through standard iOS sharing

6. Children's Privacy

SwimAlyzer is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data remains local on the device.

7. International Users

Since data is stored locally on your device, no international data transfers occur through our app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or App Store update notes.

9. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union:

  • We operate on the legal basis of contract performance and legitimate interest
  • No personal data is transferred outside your device
  • You can exercise your data rights by deleting the app

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy:

Email: feedback@swimalyzer.com
